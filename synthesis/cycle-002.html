Contingency Audit — Cycle 002

Read-mode, configurational reads, and the limits of contingent forecasting under Knightian uncertainty
Briefings 031–060 · 5 May – 19 June 2026 · 129 conditional chains · generated 2026-06-19
Cycle 2 adds the positive discipline the protocol only ever stated negatively. The break-point taxonomy says what failed; the read-mode lens says which stance failed — and the answer is unambiguous: representation broke, orientation held.

Headline

Read-mode predicts break severity. Across 129 conditional chains, every Inverted (7) and every Held-Spurious (9) outcome was a Representation- or Hybrid-read; not one Orienting-read inverted or failed at Z in the entire cycle. The model's failure is not in reading the configuration — orienting and hybrid reads share that competence — but in what it does at the terminal step: the Hybrid read collapses a live disposition into a single vector (the dominant failure: Type-V velocity × S2 narrative-over-physical), and the Representation read projects a represented template as present fact (the catastrophic failure: Type-VI × S5). The paradigm representation failure — the 037–039 cross-year template projection — is confined to one signature (S5) appearing in 12 chains, all before Briefing 040 and zero after: the 13 May factual-verification discipline eliminated it mid-cycle, proving a representation failure is correctable by explicit discipline. Velocity miscalibration is again the dominant break, as in Cycle 1, and now localizes to the hybrid read's single-vector collapse — a structural fix (name ≥2 release paths), not a calibrational one.

Theoretical Implication — Orienting vs. Knowledge Representation

The cycle is a clean empirical result for the orienting-vs-knowledge-representation theory. Under genuine flux, representation reads and the representation-tail of hybrid reads carry all the catastrophic failures, while orienting reads degrade gracefully or hold. This is the bandwidth-rotation thesis confirmed on analytical reading: a single-vector map is the trap; orienting to the disposition as a field is robust. Because the configuration read is shared across modes, the payoff is not "read better structures" but "stop converting a good configurational read into a single-vector prediction at the terminal step."

It also localizes where the LLM plausibility engine fails under Knightian uncertainty: not in configuration recognition (strong) but in resisting the pull to a single most-probable completion at the Z-step (S2 dominant at 33; S3 at 15). The S5 elimination — a named cognitive failure mode disciplined out within a cycle — is a positive control and the falsifiable claim cross-architecture testing should now probe. And the lens itself is an instance of the periodic-re-framing that keeps an analytical ensemble from recursive narrowing: a human-supplied configurational frame gave the audit its positive discipline; the machine-generated inventory validated the frame against 129 resolved instances. Neither partner produces the result alone.
