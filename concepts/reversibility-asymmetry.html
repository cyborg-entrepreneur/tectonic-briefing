Reversibility Asymmetry

META-3 · Threshold Cascade·Named in Briefing 009·13 April 2026·8 citations across 57 briefings

Canonical Definition

Physical conditions tend to irreversibility while institutional conditions tend to reversibility, so the rate at which a kinetic substrate progresses is structurally faster than the rate at which the institutional framework absorbing it can adjust. The Hormuz mine field is irreversible at physical substrate; the diplomatic agreement that would have constrained it is reversible at institutional substrate. The asymmetry drives the cascade.
