Constructive Ambiguity

META-5 · Institutional Hollowing·Named in Briefing 004·8 April 2026·20 citations across 77 briefings

Canonical Definition

When a diplomatic agreement succeeds precisely because its terms support mutually exclusive interpretations. Works only as long as nobody tests it; the testing destroys the work-doing power of the ambiguity.
