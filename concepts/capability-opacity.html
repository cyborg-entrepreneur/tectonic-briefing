Capability Opacity

META-1 · Coupling Failure·Named in Briefing 003·7 April 2026·22 citations across 66 briefings

Canonical Definition

The gap between what an AI system can do and what its operators can verify about its behavior becomes structurally unbridgeable. Capacity exists; observation does not.
