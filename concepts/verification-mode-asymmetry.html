Verification-Mode Asymmetry

META-1 · Coupling Failure·Named in Briefing 020·24 April 2026·18 citations across 75 briefings

Canonical Definition

The verification regime is structurally blind to a class of failures that only the execution regime surfaces. The DeepSeek-Mythos juxtaposition: pre-release containment cannot constrain output-distillation pathways; post-release execution surfaces failure modes the verification regime cannot.
