Commons Enclosure

META-4 · Commons Enclosure·Named in Briefing 003·7 April 2026·52 citations across 57 briefings

Canonical Definition

When a shared resource governed by collective norms is converted into a controlled access point with a fee structure and a gatekeeper. Iran's Hormuz toll booth is the canonical instance.
