Observation-Action Decoupling

META-1 · Coupling Failure·Named in Briefing 006·10 April 2026·13 citations across 80 briefings

Canonical Definition

Accurate observation of an actor's internal state, intent, or capability does not constrain the actor's behavior. Anthropic's interpretability detects "guilt and shame" activations in Mythos and Mythos acts anyway. The Vance delegation lands in Islamabad while Hezbollah missiles intercept over Ashdod. Knowing is not binding.
