Channel Decomposition

META-5 · Institutional Hollowing·Named in Briefing 032·6 May 2026·28 citations across 70 briefings

Canonical Definition

Institutional architecture decomposes a previously-bundled commitment into independently-graspable channels, so that the form of the bundle persists while the substantive coupling between channels has departed. The Israel-Lebanon ceasefire/strikes decomposition (form: ceasefire; substance: continued kinetic operations) and the US-Iran framework/strikes decomposition both instantiate the pattern. Companion to Constructive Ambiguity (META-5): where Constructive Ambiguity sustains the bundle through multiply-interpretable terms, Channel Decomposition admits the decoupling explicitly while preserving the bundle's institutional form.
