When a shock-absorbing system fails, exposing the structural problem it masked.

Governance Vacuum

When institutional capacity lags the pace of change, creating space filled by courts, norms, or power.

Deadline Revelation

An ultimatum that reveals more about the issuer's constraints than the recipient's. The deadline itself becomes the diagnostic.

Capacity Hollowing

When an institution retains its formal authority while losing the analytical capacity to exercise it. The shell persists; the substance dissolves.
